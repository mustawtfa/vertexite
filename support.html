Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account directly from within our apps (Settings > Account > Delete Account) or email us at support@vertexishere.com with your username.

Is Cortex AI free to use?

Yes, Cortex offers a free tier for everyone. We also offer premium plans for advanced features and higher limits.

Where can I download All Star?

All Star is available on the Google Play Store. An iOS version is currently in development.

How can I report a bug?

Please use the contact form above and select "Bug Report" as your subject. Include screenshots if possible.
