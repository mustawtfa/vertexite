All Star Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 27, 2025

Welcome to All Star! This Privacy Policy is specific to the All Star game ("the Game"). This document supplements our general Vertex Privacy Policy, which governs your unified Vertex Account.

1. Relationship to the General Vertex Privacy Policy

Your use of All Star is governed by both this policy and the general Vertex Privacy Policy. The general policy covers data for your core Vertex Account. This document focuses on data processed specifically for the All Star game.

2. Information We Collect Specifically for All Star

To provide a functional and competitive experience, we collect the following game-specific information:

  • Gameplay and Score Data: We track your total seasonal score and your all-time single-game high score to operate the leaderboards.
  • Public Leaderboard Data: Your Vertex username and your scores are displayed publicly on two distinct leaderboards, each showing the top 100 players: the "Seasonal Leaderboard" (total score) and the "All-Time High Score Leaderboard".
  • Technical and Gameplay Analytics: We use services like Google Firebase to collect anonymized technical and usage data (e.g., crash reports, device type, session length). This data is not personally identifiable and is used only to fix bugs, balance gameplay, and improve the service.
  • Third-Party Advertising Data: When you choose to watch an optional rewarded ad, third-party ad networks may collect information like your device advertising ID to provide personalized ads. We do not share your personal Vertex Account information with these partners.

3. How We Use and Retain Your Data

  • To Operate the Game: To run seasonal competitions, calculate rankings, display leaderboards, award badges, and synchronize your progress.
  • Historical Archive: To maintain a historical record of competition, final seasonal leaderboard data (your username and rank) will be archived by us starting from Season 8.
  • To Maintain and Improve the Service: To analyze anonymous data to make the game more fair, stable, and fun.

4. Your Data Control and Deletion Rights

Your Vertex username and game scores are shared publicly within the Game's leaderboards. We do not sell or rent your personal information.

You are in full control of your data. Deleting your unified Vertex Account via our central data deletion form at vertexishere.com/delete-data will permanently remove your username and scores from all current and historical leaderboards.

5. Children's Privacy

The All Star game has a content rating (PEGI 3) that is suitable for a general audience. However, to create a Vertex Account and use the online features of the Game (such as leaderboards and account synchronization), you must be at least 13 years old. This is a requirement of the unified Vertex Account system due to data privacy regulations.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may modify this Privacy Policy. We will indicate changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of All Star after changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
