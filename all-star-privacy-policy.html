All Star Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 30, 2025

Welcome to All Star! This Privacy Policy is specific to the All Star game ("the Game"). This document supplements our general Vertex Privacy Policy, which governs your unified Vertex Account.

1. Relationship to the General Vertex Privacy Policy

Your use of All Star is governed by both this policy and the general Vertex Privacy Policy. The general policy covers data for your core Vertex Account. This document focuses on data processed specifically for the All Star game, in alignment with our Google Play Data Safety declaration.

2. Information We Collect and Share

To provide our services, we and our third-party partners (like Google) collect and in some cases share the following types of data:

  • Personal Information (Collected, Not Shared): If you choose to create a Vertex Account for online features, we collect your username (classified as Name), Email address, and User IDs. This data collection is optional, as you can play the game offline without creating an account.
  • Approximate Location (Collected & Shared): To provide analytics and region-appropriate advertising, our partners (like Google Firebase and AdMob) automatically collect approximate location data (e.g., country or city) derived from your IP address. This data is shared with third-party advertising networks.
  • App Activity and Performance (Collected, Not Shared): We and our partners automatically collect data about how you interact with our game (App Interactions, such as scores and ad views) and how it performs on your device. This includes Crash logs and Diagnostics data. This information is used for analytics and to improve the service.
  • Device & Other IDs (Collected & Shared): When you choose to watch an optional rewarded ad, our third-party ad networks collect and share your deviceâ€™s Advertising ID to serve personalized and relevant ads and to prevent fraud.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To Operate the Game (Application Functionality & Account Management): To run seasonal competitions, calculate rankings, display leaderboards, award badges, and synchronize your progress with your account.
  • For Analytics: To analyze anonymous data to fix bugs, monitor performance, and make the game more fair, stable, and fun.
  • For Advertising & Marketing: To display optional, rewarded ads to users.
  • For Security & Fraud Prevention: To ensure the integrity of our services and advertising systems.
  • Historical Archive: To maintain a historical record of competition, final seasonal leaderboard data (your username and rank) will be archived by us starting from Season 8.

4. Your Data Control and Deletion Rights

Your Vertex username and game scores are shared publicly within the Game's leaderboards. We do not sell or rent your personal information for purposes other than serving relevant ads as described above.

You are in full control of your data. Deleting your unified Vertex Account via our central data deletion form at vertexishere.com/delete-data will permanently remove your username and scores from all current and historical leaderboards.

5. Children's Privacy

The All Star game has a content rating (PEGI 3) that is suitable for a general audience. However, to create a Vertex Account and use the online features of the Game (such as leaderboards and account synchronization), you must be at least 13 years old. This is a requirement of the unified Vertex Account system due to data privacy regulations.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may modify this Privacy Policy. We will indicate changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of All Star after changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
